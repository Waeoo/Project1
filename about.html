About this website

This website was created to share information and enlighten about the benefits and effects adaptogens provide. There are some well-researched adaptogens that has been around humans for thousands of years. You will be able to read about the different botanical names of the adaptogens, what they taste like, properties and the constituents.

The listed benefits and uses of adaptogens on this site are based on available information, including modern scientific research, records of their use in traditional medical systems, ethnobotany, and clinical observations made by practitioners. It is important to remember the following conclusions about the health benefits of adaptogens:

  1. All adaptogens have antistress qualities that help provide stabalizing effects on the neuroendocrine system, especially the HPA axis and SAS.
  2. All adaptogens help modulate and/or enhance the immune system.
  3. All adaptogens inhibit cortisol-induced mitochondrial dysfunction.

Because they have these qualities, adaptogens have been proven to help:

  • Reverse immunosuppression caused by stress
  • Reverse the decline of immune system function as people get older
  • Reduce the risk of cancer and heart disease

Information on this site is to be referenced to David Winston, RH (AHG) and Steven Maimes, book called Adaptogens.
